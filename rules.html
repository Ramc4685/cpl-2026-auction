CPL 2026 — Auction Rules

Final rules. One page. No exceptions on auction day.

1. Team purse & roster caps

ItemValue
Base team points120,000
Auction extra points20,000
Total team purse140,000
Min roster size (must reach)11
Max roster size15
Max Gold per team3
Max Silver per team5
Max Bronze per team7

2. Bid floors (opening bid by category)

CategoryFloor
Gold20,000
Silver10,000
Bronze4,000
No bid below floor. If no bid at floor, player is unsold (see §6).

3. Bid increment ladder

Current bid rangeMin increment
Up to 10,000+500
10,001 – 25,000+1,000
25,001 – 50,000+2,500
Above 50,000+5,000
Owners may jump-bid above the minimum. No fractional bids.

4. Bid eligibility (every bid must satisfy ALL)

  1. Team has remaining purse ≥ current bid.
  2. Team has reserved enough purse to fill min-roster at floor for remaining slots.
  3. Team is not at category cap (no 4th Gold, 6th Silver, 8th Bronze).
  4. Team is not at roster max (15).
Console enforces this automatically. Owner is responsible for verifying before raising hand.

5. Auction procedure (per lot)

  1. Admin announces lot: name, category, prior team, prior price (if any).
  2. Bidding opens at floor. Owner raises hand or unmutes to bid.
  3. Admin acknowledges each bid by team name + amount.
  4. "Going once, going twice, sold to [team] at [points]." Three-second pause between calls.
  5. Admin enters winner + price in console. Commit.
  6. Next lot.

6. Unsold players

  1. If no bid at floor, player goes to Unsold Pool.
  2. After main round ends, Unsold Pool is re-auctioned at floor in same order.
  3. If still unsold in round 2, player is released (cannot play CPL 2026).

7. Right to Match (RTM)

RTM is OFF. Highest bidder wins outright. Previous team has no preferential right.

8. Tie-break

Verbal auction has no ties (only one bidder can be highest). If admin is unclear which team bid first at the same level, admin's call is final.

9. Mistakes, undo & corrections

  1. Admin may undo the most recent lot before the next lot opens.
  2. Clerical console errors, including wrong player, team, amount, or accidental unsold status, may be corrected by admin as soon as discovered.
  3. Any correction must be announced to all owners and reflected in the live auction log.
  4. The verbal auction result remains final unless changed through the dispute process below.

10. Disputes

Admin's decision is final on auction day. Post-auction disputes go to owner committee within 24 hours; otherwise result stands.

11. Roles & conduct

  1. One designated bidder per team. Substitution requires admin approval.
  2. No collusion. No private side-deals on points.
  3. Mute when not bidding. Disrupting auction = warning, then forfeit current lot.
